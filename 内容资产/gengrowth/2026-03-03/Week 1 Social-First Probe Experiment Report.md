In the first week of our social-first probe experiment, we tested whether social channels can validate product-market fit signals before investing in long-form content. We launched targeted posts across Reddit, X, and LinkedIn, measuring engagement rates against pre-defined baselines. The results showed Reddit outperforming other platforms with a 7.1% engagement rate.
