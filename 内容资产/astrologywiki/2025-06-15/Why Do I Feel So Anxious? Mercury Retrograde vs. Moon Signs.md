# Why Do I Feel So Anxious? Mercury Retrograde vs. Moon Signs

Feeling jittery, overthinking every text, or waking up with a sense of dread for no reason? Before you blame the coffee, it's worth looking to the cosmos.

## In Short: The Difference

[Mercury Retrograde](/wiki/mercury) causes **mental anxiety**—think overthinking, scattered focus, and tech meltdowns. Your [Moon Sign](/wiki/moon) governs **emotional anxiety**—the deep, gut feelings of safety and attachment. Knowing whether you're dealing with a "noisy brain" or a "heavy heart" helps you choose the right relief.

## The Mercury Factor: Mental Anxiety

[Mercury](/wiki/mercury) is the planet of the mind, ruling communication, logic, and logistics. When Mercury goes retrograde, logical pathways get "tangled."

### What "Retrograde Anxiety" feels like:

- **Brain Fog:** Forgetting why you walked into a room.
- **Racing Thoughts:** Replaying a sent email over and over, looking for typos.
- **Tech Frustration:** A laptop crash leading to a disproportionate meltdown.

### Actionable Tip: Brain Dumping

Open a note or journal and spill everything in your head onto the page—unedited. Mercury anxiety eases when your brain stops trying to be the only storage system.

---

## The Moon Factor: Emotional Anxiety

While Mercury is about the head, the [Moon](/wiki/moon) is about the gut. The Moon represents your "inner child," your subconscious, and what you need to feel safe.

### What "Hard Moon Transits" feel like:

- **Heavy Heart:** A sense of loneliness or being unloved for no apparent reason.
- **Hyper-Sensitivity:** Taking a neutral comment from a friend as a personal attack.
- **Physical Need for Safety:** An irrational urge to hide under the covers (the "blanket burrito" mode).

### Actionable Tip: Self-Soothing

Lean into sensory comfort: a warm shower, soft lighting, or calming music. Emotional anxiety responds best when you communicate safety to your body first.

## Comparison Table: Is it Mercury or the Moon?

| Symptom | Mercury Retrograde | Moon Transit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary feeling** | Mental fog, scattered | Emotional weight, vulnerability |
| **Thought pattern** | Overthinking, analysis paralysis | Ruminating on feelings, past hurts |
| **Physical response** | Restlessness, fidgeting | Fatigue, need for comfort |
| **Communication** | Miscommunication, tech issues | Feeling misunderstood, withdrawn |
| **Best relief** | Write it out, organize | Sensory comfort, nurturing |
| **Duration** | ~3 weeks (retrograde period) | 2-3 days (Moon in each sign) |
