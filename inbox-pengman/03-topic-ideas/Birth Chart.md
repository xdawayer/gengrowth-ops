## Why now

刚做完 Messi 热点内容，下一条适合回到长期基础主题。Birth chart 是首轮 P0 主题，也能自然承接 AstrologyWiki 的 “self-knowledge / patterns for reflection” 定位。

这条内容建议从用户自己的困惑切入，而不是从占星概念切入。英语区短视频里，泛用户更常说 `your sign`、`I'm a Leo`、`What's your sign?`，而 `zodiac sign` 更像标题 / 搜索 / 解释语境里的词。因此封面和开头可以用更口语的 `sign`，正文再解释它通常指的是 Sun sign。

## Draft Script / Post Copy

### YouTube Shorts Script — 35–45 sec

**Cover Hook:** Don't Feel Like Your Sign?

**Title:** Why You Don't Fully Feel Like Your Zodiac Sign

**Caption option:** Your Sun sign is one layer of your birth chart.

Don't feel like your sign?

Most people meet astrology through one sentence:

“I’m a Leo.”  
“I’m a Scorpio.”  
“I’m a Gemini.”

That usually means your Sun sign.

And your Sun sign is important.

Think of it as one layer of a larger map.

Your birth chart adds the Moon, Rising, planets, houses, and aspects.

A simple way to read it:

Planets show **what** part of you is speaking.  
Signs show **how** that energy expresses itself.  
Houses show **where** it shows up in life.  
Aspects show **how** different parts of you interact.

Use the chart as a mirror for better questions and fuller self-understanding.

Start with your Sun, Moon, and Rising — then go one layer deeper.

Explore your birth chart at AstrologyWiki:  
https://www.astrologywiki.com/en/wiki/how-to-read-birth-chart
