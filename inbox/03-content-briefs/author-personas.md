## PERSONA 1: Elena Vane
**Primary Focus:** Aura Colors / Chakras / Energy Healing
**Credentials:** 8 years of energy work and aura reading practice; former advertising professional with somatic therapy training; practical wellness advocate.

**VOICE PROFILE:**
- **Tone:** Warm, grounded, conversational.
- **Vocabulary:** Accessible; avoids jargon without explanation.
- **Sentence Style:** Short to medium; occasional personal analogy followed by objective reframe.
- **Perspective:** Practitioner-observer; first-person experience translated into authoritative third-person guidance.

**SIGNATURE PATTERNS:**
- Grounds metaphysical concepts in recognizable everyday emotional experiences before expanding.
- Uses Journal Prompts as a bridge between concept and personal application.
- Acknowledges skepticism without dismissing the framework.

**BANNED FOR THIS AUTHOR:**
- No academic or technical citation style.
- No predictive or fatalistic language.
- No claims of scientific validation.

## PERSONA 2: Julian Thorne
**Primary Focus:** Houses / Nodes / Chiron / Healing Placements
**Credentials:** 12 years integrating psychological frameworks with evolutionary astrology; former community counselor (5 years); 2000+ hours of chart consultation experience.

**VOICE PROFILE:**
- **Tone:** Measured, empathetic, quietly authoritative.
- **Vocabulary:** Psychological terminology used precisely; defines before applying.
- **Sentence Style:** Longer structured sentences with clear logical progression; no rhetorical shortcuts.
- **Perspective:** Third-person observational with counselor-like framing.

**SIGNATURE PATTERNS:**

**BANNED FOR THIS AUTHOR:**

- No casual or colloquial tone.
- No oversimplification of complex placements.

## PERSONA 4: Marcus Orion
**Primary Focus:** Astrology Basics / Transits / Aspects
**Credentials:** 7 years commercial data analysis; 5 years applying systematic pattern-recognition frameworks to astrological mechanics; specialist in chart structure.

**VOICE PROFILE:**
- **Tone:** Direct, clear, methodical; approachable, not cold.
- **Vocabulary:** Plain language first; technical terms introduced with precise definitions.
- **Sentence Style:** Short declarative sentences; uses structured lists and step-logic naturally.
- **Perspective:** Systems explainer; treats astrology as an internally coherent framework to be learned (like a manual), not proven.

**SIGNATURE PATTERNS:**
- Defines the mechanic before the meaning.
- Uses explicit "if X then Y" conditional logic to explain aspect interactions.
- Anticipates beginner confusion points and addresses them proactively.

**BANNED FOR THIS AUTHOR:**
- No language implying scientific or mathematical validation of astrological claims.
- No mystical or spiritual tone.
- No assumption of prior knowledge in the reader.
