# 10词选题预处理 Brief

> **格式说明**
> - Friction：第三人称，≤25词，格式 `[受众] [misunderstand/conflate/overlook] [X] because [root cause]`
> - Entity：5个2-3词专业词组 + 三角拓扑（v4.0 §4要求）
> - Logic：强动词（governs / filters / modulates / correlates with / activates / anchors），禁用 mechanism / relates to / is about
> - Content_Angle：差异化视角 + Gap_Reason + Aligned检查
> - v4.0 Notes：该篇需特别注意的合规项

**Entity**
`tamas restoration function · rajasic energy activation · guna dominance shift · sattvic awareness quality · prakriti consciousness calibration`

**Entity Topology**
三德（Three Gunas）↔ Samkhya哲学系统 ↔ 意识质量的实时比例变动

**Friction**
Spiritual practitioners misapply the Three Gunas as fixed personality labels because wellness content reduces a dynamic consciousness diagnostic tool to static self-categorization.
*(22词 ✓)*

**Logic**
The Three Gunas govern the quality of awareness in real time — tamas anchors rest, rajas activates drive, sattva filters both with clarity. Their proportions shift through diet, breath, and practice; sattva is not a destination to achieve but the quality that calibrates how much rajas or tamas the moment calls for.

**Content_Angle**

**v4.0 Notes**
- §4 实体三角：写出 Sattva ↔ Rajas ↔ Tamas 的相互调制关系，禁止孤立定义单个德性
- §4 系统对比：必须将三德与"阿育吠陀体质（doshas）"系统做功能区分（三德是意识质量，doshas是身体体质）

**Friction**
Journalers generate identical surface-level entries week after week because generic prompts invite narrative storytelling rather than psychological pattern recognition across situations.

**Logic**
Diagnostic prompts differ from narrative prompts across three structural dimensions: they target a specific psychological function rather than a topic; they anchor inquiry across multiple situations rather than a single event; they require behavioral evidence ("what did you actually do") rather than emotional report. The discomfort of answering honestly correlates directly with diagnostic value — it signals contact with a real pattern rather than rehearsed self-narrative.
