# 一、Astrocartography Map Generator

**网址**：`/en/astrocartography-map-generator`

**Meta标题**：Astrocartography Map Generator – Free Relocation Astrology Tool | AstrologyWiki
**Meta描述**：Generate your personal astrocartography map free. Enter your birth date, time, and location to see which planetary lines run through any place on Earth.

**H1**：Astrocartography Map Generator
副标题：Enter your birth details to generate a world map showing where each planet's influence is strongest.

**工具表单**：Birth Date / Time / Location 输入框 → "Generate My Map" 按钮 → 输出地图

---

**H2：What Your Astrocartography Map Shows**
写什么：解释地图上每条线代表行星在某个经纬度升起/落下/到达中天/下中天，站在那条线附近这颗行星的主题会被放大。可以类比"出生盘回答我是谁，这张图回答我在哪里会不一样"。

**H2：The Four Line Types, Briefly**
写什么：四种线各一句话——MC线（事业曝光）/ IC线（家庭根基）/ ASC线（自我表达）/ DSC线（关系连接）。**只写一句话定向，不展开讲层级原理**，结尾加一句话+链接，引导去看专门的解读文章（链接见下方"内链"）。

**H2：What Each Planet Represents on Your Map**
写什么：Sun/Moon/Venus/Mars/Jupiter/Saturn各一句话，做成快速对照表，不展开成长段落。

**H2：Astrocartography Map Generator vs. the Full Astrocartography Guide**
写什么：一句话说明这个页面是"生成工具"，完整方法论看另一篇（链接见下方）。

**H2：Frequently Asked Questions**
1. Is this astrocartography map generator free?
2. Do I need my exact birth time to generate an accurate map?
3. What's the difference between this tool and the main Astrocartography page?
4. Can I download or share my generated map?

**内链**：
- 链接到 `/en/astrocartography`（说明性母页）
- 链接到 `/en/blog/astrocartography-interpretation`（深度解读文章，讲线型层级原理的内容放在这篇，不放在工具页里）
- 链接到 `/en/birth-chart-calculator`、`/en/solar-return-calculator`

# 二、Moon Phase Today

**网址**：`/en/moon-phase-today`

**Meta标题**：Moon Phase Today – What Moon Phase Is It Right Now? | AstrologyWiki
**Meta描述**：See today's exact moon phase and illumination percentage, updated daily. Plus a quick guide to all 8 moon phases.

**H1**：What Moon Phase Is It Today?

**首屏数据**：今日月相名称+图标、月面照亮百分比、距下次满月/新月天数。这部分是实时数据，每天会变，不是固定文案。

---

**H2：Why the Moon's Phase Changes Every Day**
写什么：月亮绕地球公转，约29.5天一个周期，8个相位轮替。固定文案，不用每天改。

**H2：The 8 Moon Phases at a Glance**
写什么：8种相位各一句话定义，做成列表或小表格。**这部分是科普全部8种，不要去判断"今天具体是哪个"**，跟首屏的动态数字分开，写完就不用再动。

**H2：Need a Different Date Instead of Today?**
写什么：一句话+链接，提示如果想查别的日期用Moon Phase Calculator。

**H2：Frequently Asked Questions**
1. What moon phase is it today?（答案写通用描述，引导看上方数据区，不要把具体相位名字写进答案里）
2. How often does the moon's phase change?
3. What's the difference between this page and the Moon Phase Calculator?
4. Where can I check the moon phase for a different date?

**内链**：
- 链接到 `/en/moon-phase-calculator`（查别的日期）
- 链接到 `/en/birth-chart-calculator`
