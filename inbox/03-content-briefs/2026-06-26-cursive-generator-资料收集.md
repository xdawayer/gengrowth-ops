## 三、技术原理（原文）

**核心机制：**
"Unicode has thousands of characters that look like styled versions of the Latin alphabet. The 'cursive script' style maps each letter to a character in the Mathematical Alphanumeric Symbols block — U+1D49C through U+1D4CF, roughly."

"When you install a font file (like Arial.ttf or Lobster.otf) on your computer, only you can see it. Unicode characters, conversely, are text-based and universally readable."

**为什么可以跨平台复制粘贴：**
"Your phone or browser sees these as regular text characters, not formatting, which is why they survive copy-paste across platforms. When you paste 𝓱𝓮𝓵𝓵𝓸 into Instagram, the platform sees it as plain text, just like the letters a-b-c, but from a different section of the Unicode table."

**兼容性说明：**
"The cursive styles specifically have pretty good support across iOS, Android, Windows, and Mac at this point, but some of the more decorative styles can show up as empty boxes on older Android phones."

"Minor rendering differences occur across platforms. A 'cursive T looking slightly thicker or sporting a more pronounced flourish on an iPhone compared to a Google Pixel' represents normal typography variation."

**历史背景（CapitalizeMyTitle 原文）：**
"In English, cursive writing was originally developed in the 17th century to speed up the writing process since writers did not need to lift their quills until the end of a word."
